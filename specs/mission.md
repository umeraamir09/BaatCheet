# BaatCheet Mission

## North Star
**Zero Lag. Zero Compromise. Maximum Performance.**

Every decision, feature, and line of code in BaatCheet must answer: "Does this reduce latency or resource usage?" If the answer is no, it does not belong in BaatCheet.

## Core Pillars
1. **Performance Above All**: The app must be invisible while gaming. It should use minimal CPU, RAM, and network bandwidth.
2. **Reliability**: Calls and messages must not drop. The app must recover gracefully from network issues.
3. **Privacy**: All calls and chats are encrypted end-to-end. No data mining. No unnecessary data collection.
4. **Minimalism**: We ship only what our users need. No bloat. No feature creep.
5. **Speed of Communication**: Text and voice must feel instantaneous. No perceptible delay in conversations.

## What We Are Not
- We are not Discord.
- We do not need to scale to millions of users.
- We do not need every feature under the sun.

## User Promise
"A fast, reliable, and secure space for you and your friends to talk without your game noticing."
