# Phase 2: The Texting Backbone - Validation

## Success Criteria
1. **Real-time Messaging**: Two clients can log in, create a server/channel, and send messages in real-time.
2. **Direct Messages**: Two clients can initiate a DM thread and send messages to each other.
3. **Message Features**: Users can edit their sent messages and reply to specific messages in the thread.
4. **Performance**: Everything must happen with zero perceptible lag. The app should not feel sluggish when sending or receiving a rapid succession of messages.
5. **No Breakages**: Ensure Phase 1 (Walking Skeleton) features like Clerk authentication are still working as intended.
